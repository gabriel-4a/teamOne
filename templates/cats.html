Our website offers easy assessible information on how to take care of your pet. We believe all pets should be treated with care and raised in a healthy environment.

Basics :Cats are lovely pets, and are very calm conpanions. They need lots of care. Cats need lots of attention and a calm environment, which ranks their difficulty at hard.
Food :Cats should be fed one bowl a day according to their size. They should eat dry or wet commercial cat foods. Try to avoid human food, and give thems snacks meant for cats.
Needs : Cats do not often need to be bathed often. Every 8 weeks should be good for a cat. Cats also need a litter box. Every cat needs to be spayed or nutered as well .
DANGERS : There are many things that are harmful to a cat, but be especially careful with what you feed your cat. You should never feed your cat avocado, grapes, rasisns, or chocolates. This will cause serious pain for your cat, and maybe even death. You should also avoid giving your cat milk. It may seem cute, but cats are lactose intolerant.
