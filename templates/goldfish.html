Our website offers easy assessible information on how to take care of your pet. We believe all pets should be treated with care and raised in a healthy environment.

Basics : Goldfish are a wonderful starter pet. They do not require heavy care or attention ranking them as an easy pet to take care of.
Food : Goldfish can eat commercial goldfish flakes. They should not be fed any more than a small pinch of the flakes.
Needs : Goldfish will need a water filter. You will need to change the water about once a month.
DANGERS : Goldfish should not be fed any human food at all. They also need clean water, so do not put anything in their tank other than decorations or their food.
