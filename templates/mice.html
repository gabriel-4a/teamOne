Our website offers easy assessible information on how to take care of your pet. We believe all pets should be treated with care and raised in a healthy environment.

Basics :Mouse are very small, but need a bit of attention which ranks their difficulty at medium.
Food : Mice should eat commercial mouse food. You can give them tiny amounts of non citrus vegetables and fruits.
Needs : Mice require a nice clean cage with bedding,toys, and a water bottle feeder. They do not need to be bathed, but require a light sponge cleaning every 1-2 months. Mice usally keep themselves clean.
DANGERS : You should never feed your mouse choclate. This will cause serious pain for your mouse, and maybe even death.
