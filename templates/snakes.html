Our website offers easy assessible information on how to take care of your pet. We believe all pets should be treated with care and raised in a healthy environment.

Basics : Snakes are very cool pets. Snakes require some attention, which ranks their difficulty at medium. This page is specifically based off of Corn Snakes
Food : Corn Snakes must be fed once a week. They usally eat raw defrosted mice. This is the most humane way to feed snakes
Needs : Snakes will require a heatlamp for them to stay warm.
DANGERS : Snakes should not be fed human food. Snakes should also not be kept in a cold environment.
